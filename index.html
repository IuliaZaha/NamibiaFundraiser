BRING HOPE TO NAMIBIA

We're going back!

Last year, we traveled to Namibia for 2 weeks. During that time, we ran educative workshops, Sunday School, and practical outreaches for all ages and more. No one left out. But the relationships we created, both with kids and adults need to be worked on and strengthened if we want to succeed. Because lasting change doesn't happen at once.

For this purpose, we are going back in February 2027!

About Our Trip

Hi, I'm Julia!

How We're Helping

Local projects we’re supporting while in Namibia.

Project 20

A 6-month project helping families break the cycle of poverty through goal setting and mentorship.

SuperWoman

A girls-only program offering a safe space to grow emotionally, spiritually, and socially.

Kids Club

A weekly safe space for over 400 children to learn, play, and be spiritually uplifted.

Youth Club

Focused on teens and young adults, empowering them to become leaders in their communities.
